## INTRODUCTION

Alright everyone, today you are not just students .. you are scientific investigators.

We have a case.

A mysterious organism has been found dead in a tropical forest. The cause of death is unknown, but there is one key detail… there appears to be a fungus involved.

Your job is to analyze the evidence and determine which fungal species is responsible.

You will receive a list of suspects. As we move through the investigation, new evidence will be revealed. Each piece of evidence should help narrow down the possibilities.

The goal is to use scientific reasoning to identify the correct organism.

All suspects belong to the phylum Ascomycota, but they differ in their biology, ecology, and host interactions.

Let’s begin the investigation.
